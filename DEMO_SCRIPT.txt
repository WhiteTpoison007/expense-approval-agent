[1:00 - ARCHITECTURE]
(show assets/architecture_diagram.png)
Let's walk through the architecture. When a user submits an expense request, the payload first hits our Security Checkpoint. If it passes validation, it goes to our main Orchestrator Agent. The Orchestrator leverages our custom MCP Server to check role limits, retrieve past billing history, and query vendor risk watchlists. Next, the formatted details are parsed by a Report Formatter Agent into a structured schema. Finally, our Decision Router checks the results. Low-value, compliant claims are auto-approved, while higher-value or flagged claims are suspended and routed to the Human Approval node for manager confirmation.
